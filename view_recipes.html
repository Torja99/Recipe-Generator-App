    Spiced Chicken and Tomato Rice

    You are cooking for this many people:


    You are cooking on:


      Ingredients:

    • 2 boneless chicken breasts
    • 1 medium sized tomato
    • 3 cups of rice
    • optional : Oil, Paprika, Cumin, Ginger

      Steps to Cook:

    1. Cut chicken into strips, and dice tomato
    2. Heat pan to medium high, pour 5tbsp of oil, place spices
    3. Place tomatoes and chicken soon after
    4. Cook till chicken is golden brown
